Mission and Vision


We provide Safe and Reliable Transportation for a wide range of chemicals within Europe, delivering full satisfaction to our customers and long term profitability to our shareholders. Our vision is to be the automatic first choice carrier for producers and receivers of shipping chemicals within the Baltic, North West Europe and the Mediterranean and through constant focus on the quality of our service, to remain their automatic first choice.


Our Policy


Safety

Throughout every part of our operation we put Safety and Environmental care on the top of our agenda. There are no commercial or other circumstances which will make us compromise our approach to either.

Our Customer

Our Customers’ requirements, and our obligation to fulfill them to their absolute satisfaction are fundamental to our vision. We know that our success depends on our customers, and we want them to look upon us as the strongest link in their supply chain.


Our Assets

Our floating Assets – our ships. We are determined to maintain a fleet of the highest standard. We are committed to investing in the future in order to maintain our reputation for quality and innovation.


Human Resources

Our other assets – Human Resources. We value our colleagues, both ashore and afloat. We recognize their importance, and we demonstrate that by delegating and by providing training and beneficial terms. We trust our colleagues, and we foster a working atmosphere of mutual respect.


Ethical Standard

The importance of maintaining high Ethical Standards in the way we do business. We negotiate in a straightforward and honest way, and we deliver what we promise. We adhere to corporate values which prioritise honesty and integrity, we follow an anti trust compliance program and we apply best business pracitices.
